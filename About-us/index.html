Our Story

Founded in 2015, Diamond Credit has grown from a small local lender to one of Uganda's most trusted financial service providers. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide accessible and responsible financial solutions to individuals and businesses.

2015

Founded in Kampala with our first branch

2018

Expanded to 5 branches across Central Uganda

2021

Launched digital lending platform

2023

Served over 50,000 satisfied customers
